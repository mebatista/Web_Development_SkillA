Frequently Asked Questions

What are the library opening hours?

Our library is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How do I get a library card?

To get a library card, you need to visit our library with a valid photo ID and proof of address. Our staff will assist you in filling out a registration form, and you'll receive your library card on the spot.

Can I borrow books if I'm not a member?

Yes, you can borrow books as a guest user by presenting a valid photo ID. However, guest users have limited borrowing privileges compared to registered members.

How many books can I borrow at once?

Registered members can borrow up to 5 books at a time. However, certain categories of books may have different borrowing limits.
